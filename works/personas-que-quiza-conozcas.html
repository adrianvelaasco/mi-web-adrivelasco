PERSONAS QUE QUIZÁ CONOZCAS

Personas Que Quizá Conozcas. 2025

A collaborative composition by Adri Velasco, Fran Escobar, Alejandro Mondragón, and Alberto Hijón.

This piece explores the connections and disconnections in the digital age, represented through a multi-layered acoustic and electronic performance.

Composers Adri Velasco, Fran Escobar, Alejandro Mondragón, Alberto Hijón
Performer Gabriel Brockway
Musicians Violeta Casado, Javier Díaz, Paula Ortega, María Calvo
